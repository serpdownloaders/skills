## Why DeviantArt Downloader

DeviantArt hosts millions of artworks across a wide range of categories, from digital illustrations and concept art to photography and traditional media scans. While the platform allows artists to share their creations publicly, saving those images at full resolution is not always straightforward. Many deviations display a scaled-down preview in the browser, and accessing the original file often requires navigating through multiple page layers or dealing with download restrictions set by the artist. Right-clicking and saving frequently yields a lower-quality version or a thumbnail rather than the full artwork.

DeviantArt Downloader is being built to solve this problem at the browser level. The extension will detect artwork on the page, locate the highest-quality version available, and let you save it with a single action. For users who want to download multiple pieces from a gallery or collection, the planned batch mode will handle queuing and organizing files so you do not have to repeat the process for every individual deviation.

## Planned Features

- One-click download of full-resolution artwork from any deviation page
- Batch downloading from artist galleries, featured folders, and collections
- Automatic detection of the highest available image resolution
- Filename formatting using artist name, deviation title, and ID
- Support for multiple image formats including PNG, JPG, and GIF
- Download queue management for handling large gallery exports
- Folder organization options to sort downloads by artist or category
- Cross-browser compatibility targeting Chrome, Edge, Brave, and Firefox

## How It Will Work

1. Install the extension once it is released from the browser extension store.
2. Navigate to DeviantArt and browse to any deviation, gallery, or artist profile.
3. Open a deviation page to view the artwork you want to save.
4. Click the extension icon or use the in-page download button that appears on the artwork.
5. The extension detects the highest-resolution version of the image available.
6. Select individual pieces or toggle batch mode to queue multiple artworks.
7. Choose a destination folder or use the default download directory.
8. Start the download and the file saves to your local machine at full quality.

## Supported Content Types

- Digital illustrations and paintings in raster formats (PNG, JPG)
- Traditional art scans and mixed media uploads
- Photography and photomanipulation works

Downloaded files will retain their original format and resolution as provided by the artist on DeviantArt. The extension will not convert between formats unless a future feature specifically addresses format preferences.

## FAQ

**When will DeviantArt Downloader be released?**

**Will it download images at full resolution?**
The extension is designed to locate and save the highest-resolution version of the artwork that DeviantArt makes available. The actual resolution depends on what the artist uploaded and any platform-level restrictions.

**Can I download from private or restricted galleries?**
The extension will only be able to access content that is visible to your browser session. If a gallery or deviation requires a login or specific permissions, you will need to be signed in with appropriate access.

**Does it support batch downloads?**
Yes, batch downloading from galleries and collections is a planned core feature. You will be able to select multiple artworks and queue them for download in a single operation.

**Is it free?**

**Will downloaded files include metadata about the artwork?**
The plan is to preserve relevant information such as the artist name and deviation title in the filename. Embedding additional metadata into the image file itself is being evaluated as a potential feature.

## About DeviantArt

DeviantArt is one of the largest online art communities in the world, founded in 2000 and home to over 60 million registered users. The platform hosts hundreds of millions of original artworks spanning digital art, traditional art, photography, literature, and more. Artists use DeviantArt to share their portfolios, connect with fans, sell prints, and participate in community groups. Despite its rich visual library, the platform does not offer a built-in bulk download tool or a streamlined way to save full-resolution images for offline use. DeviantArt Downloader is being developed to fill that gap for users who want convenient, organized access to artwork they discover on the platform.
