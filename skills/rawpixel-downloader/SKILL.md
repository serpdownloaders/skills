## Why Rawpixel Downloader

Rawpixel hosts an extensive library of creative resources spanning stock photography, vector illustrations, PSD mockups, and curated public domain collections. Downloading assets from the platform typically involves opening individual resource pages, selecting license types, choosing resolutions, and saving files one at a time. For designers and content creators who regularly pull assets from Rawpixel, this repetitive workflow adds up quickly.

Rawpixel Downloader is being engineered to integrate directly into the browser so it can identify available assets on any Rawpixel page, surface download options in a compact popup, and handle file retrieval without forcing you through each resource page individually. The aim is to reduce the friction between finding a creative asset and having it saved locally and ready for use in your project.

## Planned Features

- Automatic detection of downloadable assets on Rawpixel gallery and detail pages
- One-click saving of individual stock photos, vectors, and illustrations
- Batch download mode for selecting and exporting multiple assets at once
- Resolution and format selection when multiple variants are available
- Filename preservation using original Rawpixel asset titles and identifiers
- Download queue with progress tracking for large batch operations
- Browser-native workflow requiring no external desktop applications
- Cross-browser compatibility targeting Chrome, Edge, Brave, and Firefox

## How It Will Work

1. Install the extension once it is released.
2. Navigate to Rawpixel and browse their library of creative assets.
3. Open a gallery page, collection, or individual asset detail page.
4. Click the extension icon to scan the current page for available resources.
5. Review the list of detected assets in the extension popup.
6. Select individual files or use batch mode to check multiple items.
7. Choose your preferred resolution or format if options are presented.
8. Start the download and the files will be saved to your local machine.

## Expected Formats

- Input: Rawpixel asset pages containing photos, vectors, PSD files, and public domain art
- Output: JPEG, PNG, SVG, PSD, or AI files depending on the source asset and its available formats

Downloaded files will retain their original format and quality level wherever possible, ensuring compatibility with standard design tools such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma, and Canva.

## About Rawpixel

Rawpixel is a creative resource platform that provides a large catalog of stock photos, vector graphics, PSD mockups, and public domain artwork sourced from museums and cultural institutions worldwide. The platform serves designers, marketers, and content creators with both free and premium tiers, but does not offer a native bulk download or browser-integrated export tool. Rawpixel Downloader is being built to fill that gap by giving users a faster, more efficient way to save the creative assets they need directly from within their browser.
