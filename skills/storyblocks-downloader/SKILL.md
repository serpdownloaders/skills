## Why Storyblocks Downloader

Storyblocks offers a massive library of royalty-free stock media under a subscription model, but the download process on the platform itself can feel repetitive when you are pulling multiple assets for a single project. Each clip or track requires its own set of clicks — selecting a resolution, confirming the license, waiting for the file to prepare, and then saving it. When you are building a video timeline or scoring a project with several audio tracks, that overhead adds up fast.

Storyblocks Downloader is being designed to sit inside the browser and reduce that friction. The goal is to detect Storyblocks asset pages and search results in the active tab, let you pick what to save and in which format, and handle the download flow in fewer steps so you spend more time editing and less time managing files.

## Planned Features

- One-click downloads from individual Storyblocks asset pages
- Batch download support from search results, collections, and folder views
- Resolution and format selection for video assets before saving
- Audio quality and format options for music and sound effect downloads
- Automatic filename generation using asset titles and metadata
- Download queue so you can stack multiple assets without waiting for each one
- Folder organization options to sort downloads by media type or project
- Browser-native workflow with no external software dependencies
- Cross-browser compatibility targeting Chrome, Edge, Brave, and Firefox

## How It Will Work

1. Install the extension once it is released.
2. Open Storyblocks in your browser and sign in to your subscription account.
3. Browse or search for the video clips, audio tracks, images, or templates you need.
4. Use the extension controls to select assets you want to download.
5. Choose resolution, format, or quality options where applicable.
6. Confirm the download and the extension handles the rest.
7. Files are saved to your local machine, organized by your chosen folder structure.
8. Review your download history in the extension popup to track what you have already saved.

## Expected Formats

- Video: MP4, MOV — resolution options depending on what Storyblocks provides for your plan tier
- Audio: MP3, WAV — quality levels based on the available source files
- Images: JPEG, PNG — standard web and print resolutions
- Templates: After Effects, Premiere Pro, and other project file formats as offered by Storyblocks

Exported files will retain their original quality and licensing metadata so you can track usage across projects.

## About Storyblocks

Storyblocks is a subscription-based stock media library that provides unlimited downloads of royalty-free video footage, audio tracks, images, and design templates. It serves creators across film, marketing, social media, and education with a library of over a million assets. The platform offers individual and team plans, each with different access tiers for HD, 4K, and premium content. Storyblocks Downloader is being built to make the asset acquisition step faster and more organized for subscribers who regularly pull media from the platform into their editing workflows.
