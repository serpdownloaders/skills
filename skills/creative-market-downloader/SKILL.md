## Why Creative Market Downloader

Creative Market hosts a vast catalog of premium design assets from independent creators, but the process of retrieving purchases can be surprisingly slow. Each asset must be downloaded individually from your purchase history, and if you have accumulated dozens or hundreds of items over months or years, re-downloading them after a system migration or hard drive failure becomes a manual chore. There is no built-in way to batch-download, filter by file type, or quickly locate a specific font family buried in a long transaction list.

Creative Market Downloader is being designed to solve that friction. The extension will sit inside your browser, read your purchase history on Creative Market, and give you tools to search, filter, and download assets without repeatedly navigating back and forth through the marketplace. Instead of hunting through order confirmation emails or scrolling through pages of past purchases, you will be able to pull up everything from a single panel and grab exactly the files you need.

For designers who rely on Creative Market assets in daily client work, this kind of workflow improvement adds up. Fewer clicks to get the right font file, less confusion about which license covers which asset, and a centralized view of everything you have already paid for.

## How It Will Work

1. Install the extension once it is released.
2. Navigate to Creative Market and sign in to your account.
3. Open the extension popup to load your purchase history.
4. Browse, search, or filter your purchased assets by type or date.
5. Select the individual assets or groups of assets you want to download.
6. Choose a destination folder or use the default download location.
7. Start the download and the extension will retrieve each file.
8. Review the download summary to confirm all files were saved.

## FAQ

**Will it let me download assets I have not purchased?**
No. The extension will only work with assets tied to your Creative Market account through completed purchases. It does not bypass any purchase or licensing requirements.

**Does it work with Creative Market's free goods?**
Yes. Free goods that you have claimed and that appear in your purchase history will be accessible through the extension just like any paid asset.

**Will it preserve license information?**
The plan is to surface license type and usage terms for each asset so you can verify your rights before using a file in a project.

## About Creative Market

Creative Market is an online marketplace where independent designers sell digital assets including fonts, graphics, templates, website themes, stock photography, and 3D models. It connects creators with buyers looking for high-quality design resources, offering both individual purchases and a subscription model. Creative Market does not provide a bulk download tool or a browser-based asset manager for past purchases, which is the gap Creative Market Downloader is being built to fill.
