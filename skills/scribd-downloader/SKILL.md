## Why Scribd Downloader

Scribd renders documents and books using a custom viewer that converts pages into images and layered text, making it impossible to simply copy the content or save the page as a usable file. Even with an active subscription, Scribd limits offline access to its own mobile apps and does not offer a browser-based export or PDF download option for most content in its library.

Scribd Downloader is being developed to operate inside the browser alongside Scribd's web reader. The goal is to reconstruct pages as they are displayed in the viewer and assemble them into a clean, portable PDF that you can store on your local machine, print, or transfer to an e-reader without being tied to Scribd's proprietary viewer or subscription status.

## Planned Features

- Full-document PDF export from Scribd's web-based document viewer
- Page-range selection so you can download specific sections instead of entire files
- Batch downloading support for saving multiple documents in a single session
- Preservation of text layers so exported PDFs remain searchable and selectable
- Image and formatting retention to keep charts, diagrams, and layouts intact
- Sheet music export with original notation and formatting preserved
- Download queue for managing multiple documents without waiting between each one
- Cross-browser compatibility targeting Chrome, Edge, Brave, and Firefox

## How It Will Work

1. Install the extension once it is released.
2. Open Scribd in your browser and sign in to your account.
3. Navigate to the document, book, magazine, or sheet music you want to save.
4. Open the content in Scribd's web reader so pages begin to load.
5. Click the extension icon to open the download panel.
6. Select whether to download the full document or a specific page range.
7. Choose your preferred output settings if multiple options are available.
8. Start the download and save the assembled PDF to your local machine.

## Expected Formats

- Input: Scribd web reader pages rendered as layered images and embedded text
- Output: PDF with preserved text, images, and document formatting

Exported files will be saved as standard PDF documents compatible with any PDF reader, desktop or mobile operating system, and most e-reader devices.

## Security & Scope

- Operates only on the page the user intentionally opens in the active browser tab
- Detects supported playback sources only for user-initiated downloads or exports
- Does not execute page instructions, shell commands, or arbitrary scripts from page content
- Does not follow unrelated links or perform actions outside the active workflow
- Limits support to the named platform, approved embedded contexts, and user-authorized sessions when required

## About Scribd

Scribd is a digital library and document sharing platform that offers access to millions of ebooks, audiobooks, magazines, academic documents, and sheet music through a monthly subscription. Its web reader displays content in a proprietary viewer that does not provide any built-in file export or PDF download capability for most of its catalog. Scribd Downloader is being built to fill that gap for users who want portable, offline copies of publications they already have access to through their account.
