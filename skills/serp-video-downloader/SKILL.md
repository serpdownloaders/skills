## Troubleshooting

- **“No downloadable formats found”**: The site may block downloads, or the video is protected. Try another source or check permissions/availability.
- **“Quality not available”**: If the source doesn’t provide 4K/HD, choose the next best resolution.
- **“Subtitles missing”**: Subtitles must exist at the source to be exported (SRT/VTT).
- **“Audio extraction failed”**: Some sources restrict audio-only formats; try MP4/WEBM first.
- **“License not recognized”**: Re-enter email and license key. Ensure you’re online for activation.
- **“Slow download”**: Network conditions or rate-limits can apply. Pause/batch to off-peak times.
