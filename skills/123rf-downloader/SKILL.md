## Why 123RF Downloader

123RF hosts millions of stock photos, vectors, editorial images, and video clips. After you purchase or license an asset, actually downloading it still means clicking through modals, selecting sizes, and confirming formats — often one file at a time. If you need to pull multiple assets from a search page or your download history, it becomes repetitive.

123RF Downloader is being built to sit inside the browser and simplify that last step. Browse or search on 123RF, and the extension will detect licensed assets on the page so you can save them directly without the extra clicks.

## Planned Features

- Detect licensed stock photos, vectors, and clips on 123RF pages
- One-click download for individual assets from search results or detail pages
- Batch selection for downloading multiple assets from a single page
- Original resolution and format preservation for photos and vectors
- Video clip export in the highest available quality
- Download history tracking so you can see what you have already saved
- Metadata tagging with title, contributor, and license type where available
- Cross-browser support targeting Chrome, Edge, Brave, and Firefox

## How It Will Work

1. Install the extension once it is released.
2. Sign in to your 123RF account in the browser.
3. Browse or search for assets as you normally would.
4. The extension detects licensed assets visible on the page.
5. Click the download button on individual assets or select multiple.
6. Choose your preferred resolution or format when options are available.
7. Files save directly to your local Downloads folder.

## Expected Formats

- Photos: JPEG at original upload resolution
- Vectors: EPS or AI source files where available, plus JPEG preview
- Video clips: MP4 in the highest licensed resolution

Downloaded files will match the formats 123RF delivers for your license tier, preserved without re-encoding or quality loss.
