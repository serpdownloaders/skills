## Why Dreamstime Downloader

Dreamstime hosts over 200 million stock assets including photographs, vector graphics, illustrations, editorial content, and video clips. When users purchase or license content on the platform, downloading those files often involves navigating through individual asset pages, selecting the correct resolution or license tier, and repeating the process for every item in a project batch. There is no native bulk download workflow built into the browser experience, and managing large volumes of licensed assets across different collections can become tedious.

Dreamstime Downloader is being built to sit directly in the browser and integrate with the Dreamstime website. The goal is to detect the assets available under your account, surface the correct licensed versions, and let you save them to your local machine without bouncing between tabs and download confirmation screens. It keeps the entire workflow inside the browser where you are already browsing and selecting content.

## Planned Features

- One-click download of licensed stock photos at your purchased resolution
- Vector and illustration export in original file formats such as EPS and AI
- Editorial image downloads with associated license metadata preserved
- Video clip saving for licensed footage tied to your Dreamstime account
- Batch download mode for grabbing multiple assets from a collection page
- Automatic file naming based on asset title, ID, or contributor information

- Cross-browser compatibility targeting Chrome, Edge, Brave, and Firefox

## How It Will Work

1. Install the extension once it is released.
2. Open the Dreamstime website and sign in to your account.
3. Browse to the asset, collection, or lightbox you want to download from.
4. Open the extension popup to see your licensed assets on the current page.
5. Select individual items or use batch mode to queue multiple assets.
6. Confirm the resolution and file format for each selected download.
7. Choose a destination folder or use the default download directory.
8. Start the download and the files will save directly to your machine.

## Expected Formats

- Photos: JPEG at the resolution matching your purchased license tier
- Vectors and illustrations: EPS, AI, or SVG depending on the original upload
- Editorial images: JPEG with embedded license metadata where available
- Video clips: MP4 or MOV based on the source format provided by Dreamstime

All downloaded files will retain their original quality and format as supplied by the Dreamstime platform. No re-encoding or compression is applied by the extension.

## FAQ

**When will Dreamstime Downloader be released?**
A release date has not been set. Sign up at the waitlist link above to be notified as soon as it is available.

**Does it work with free Dreamstime assets?**
The extension is being designed primarily around licensed and purchased content tied to your account. Support for free assets may be considered depending on how Dreamstime structures access to them.

**Will it download assets I have not purchased?**
No. The extension will only facilitate downloading assets that are already licensed or purchased under your Dreamstime account. It does not bypass any licensing restrictions.

**What image resolutions will it support?**
The available resolution will match the license tier you purchased. If you bought a small-web license, the extension will download at that resolution. Higher tiers will unlock larger file sizes.

**Is it free?**
Pricing details will be announced closer to launch. SERP extensions typically include a free trial period.

**Can I download video clips as well as photos?**
Yes. Video clip support for licensed footage is a planned feature alongside photos, vectors, illustrations, and editorial content.

## About Dreamstime

Dreamstime is a stock photography and royalty-free media platform founded in 2000, offering access to over 200 million creative assets. Its library spans professional photographs, vector graphics, illustrations, editorial images, and video clips contributed by photographers and artists worldwide. The platform serves individual creators, agencies, and enterprises through various subscription and credit-based licensing models. Dreamstime Downloader is being built to simplify the process of saving licensed assets from the platform directly through the browser, reducing the friction between selecting content and getting files onto your local machine.
